## FAQ Section

### Getting Started

**Q: Is Recruiting Compass really free?**
Yes. All core features are free with no credit card required. We're free during the MVP phase to help families navigate recruiting without barriers.

**Q: Do I need a coach or advisor to use it?**
No. Recruiting Compass is built for athletes and families to use independently. Coaches and advisors can recommend it, but it's designed for self-service.

**Q: Can I use this on my phone?**
Yes. Recruiting Compass is mobile-responsive—use it on desktop, tablet, or phone. Native mobile apps are coming in 2027.

### Features

**Q: How is my fit score calculated?**
Your fit score (1-10 scale) is based on:
- Academic alignment (your GPA vs. school's standards)
- Athletic alignment (your stats vs. team roster)
- Location preference match
- Program performance and reputation
- Scholarship availability
- Coaching philosophy fit

The more info you add to your profile, the more accurate your fit scores.

**Q: Can my parent see my information?**
Only if you invite them. Parents get a read-only view of your schools, coaches, interactions, and timeline. You stay in full control.

**Q: What if a coach doesn't respond?**
We'll alert you and suggest a recovery plan: the best timing and approach to re-engage them. It's your choice whether to follow up.

### Privacy & Security

**Q: Is my data safe?**
Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices. Your data is never shared with coaches or third parties unless you choose to share it.

**Q: Can coaches see that I'm tracking them?**
No. Coaches don't know you're using Recruiting Compass. You stay in control of your own data.

**Q: What happens to my data if I leave?**
Your data is yours. You can export your information anytime or delete your account.

### Recruiting Process

**Q: What if I'm not sure which division level to target?**
That's okay. Start by adding schools you're interested in. Your fit scores will help you understand which divisions are realistic matches.

**Q: When should I start recruiting?**
Many athletes start as freshmen (age 14), but it's never too late. If you're a junior or senior, you can catch up by logging past interactions and focusing on active outreach.

**Q: How many schools should I track?**
Most athletes track 10-20 schools across all tiers:
- 2-3 reach schools (dream schools, long shot)
- 4-6 target schools (realistic matches)
- 2-3 safety schools (likely accepts)

**Q: How often should I update my information?**
Update your profile as your stats improve (GPA, athletic performance). Log interactions with coaches within 24 hours while they're fresh. Review your timeline weekly.
