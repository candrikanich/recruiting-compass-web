## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Sign Up

1. Go to [recruitingcompass.com](https://recruitingcompass.com)
2. Click **"Sign Up"** in the top right
3. Enter your email address
4. Create a password (8+ characters)
5. Click **"Create Account"**

You'll receive a confirmation email. Click the link to verify your email.

### Step 2: Select Your Role

After email verification, choose your account type:

- **I'm a Student-Athlete** (Athlete) - You're the player managing your recruiting
- **I'm a Parent/Guardian** (Parent) - You're supporting a student-athlete

**Recommendation:** Start with the athlete account. Parents can be invited later.

### Step 3: Complete Your Basic Info

Enter:
- Full name
- High school
- Graduation year (senior class year)
- Primary position

Don't worryâ€”you can update this anytime. Click **"Continue to Dashboard"** to proceed.

## Adding Your First School

Schools are the colleges you're targeting. You'll track coaches, fit, and interactions at each school.

### How to Add a School

1. From your **Dashboard**, click the **"Schools"** tab (or "Add School")
2. Click **"+ Add School"**
3. **Search for your school:**
   - Start typing the school name (e.g., "University of Florida")
   - Select from the dropdown list
4. **Set your Priority Tier:**
   - **Reach Schools**: Dream schools (long shot)
   - **Target Schools**: Realistic matches
   - **Safety Schools**: Likely admits
   - *Choose one; you can change later*
5. **Click "Add School"**

You'll see your school appear in the list with:
- School logo
- Location
- Division level
- Your fit score (builds over time)

### Add 2-3 More Schools (Optional)

Repeat the process for your top 2-3 target schools. Most athletes track 10-20 schools across all tiers.

## Adding Your First Coach

Coaches are the people recruiting you at each school. Track interactions with each coach to build a timeline of relationships.

### How to Add a Coach

1. From your **Dashboard**, go to the **"Schools"** or **"Coaches"** tab
2. **Find your school** and click on it
3. Click **"Add Coach"** (or **"+ Coach"**)
4. **Enter coach information:**
   - Name (e.g., "John Smith")
   - Position/Title (e.g., "Head Coach" or "Recruiting Coordinator")
   - Email address
   - Phone number (optional)
5. **Click "Add Coach"**

You'll see the coach's card appear with:
- Contact info
- Interaction history (empty for now)
- Responsiveness score (builds as you interact)

### Quick Tip

Search online for your target school's baseball coaching staff. Most schools have rosters on their athletic department websites.

## Logging Your First Interaction

An interaction is any communication with a coach: email, phone call, text, camp attendance, visit, etc.

### How to Log an Interaction

1. **Find the coach** you want to log an interaction for
2. Click on the **coach's name/card**
3. Click **"+ Log Interaction"** or **"Add Interaction"**
4. **Fill in the details:**
   - **Type**: Email, Phone Call, Text, In-Person, Showcase, Visit, etc.
   - **Date**: When did it happen? (defaults to today)
   - **Notes**: What was discussed? (Keep it brief)
     - Example: "Introduced myself via email. Shared highlight video link."
   - **Outcome** (optional): "Positive," "Neutral," "No Response"
5. **Click "Log Interaction"**

### You'll See:

- The interaction appears on the coach's interaction history timeline
- A timestamp showing when it happened
- Your notes for future reference

## Inviting a Parent

Want a parent or guardian to see your recruiting progress? Invite them to your account.

### How to Invite a Parent

1. Go to **Settings** (gear icon, top right)
2. Select **"Account & Collaboration"**
3. Click **"Invite Parent"**
4. Enter their email address
5. Click **"Send Invite"**

They'll receive an email with an invite link. Once they accept, they can:
- View your schools, coaches, and interactions
- See your timeline progress
- Receive notifications about milestones
- Post comments on interactions (family discussion)

**Note:** Parents can view but not edit your dataâ€”you stay in control.

## Key Concepts

### Timeline Phases

Your recruiting journey follows a 4-year timeline:

| Phase | Grade | Focus |
|-------|-------|-------|
| **Freshman** | 9th grade | Foundation: Build skills, get on coaches' radars |
| **Sophomore** | 10th grade | Active Outreach: Attend showcases, send intro emails |
| **Junior** | 11th grade | Peak Recruiting: Campus visits, build relationships |
| **Senior** | 12th grade | Decision: Narrow choices, commit |

You can advance phases anytime, but they're designed to guide you through the recruiting timeline naturally.

### Fit Score (1-10 Scale)

A score showing how well a school matches your academic, athletic, and personal goals.

- **9-10**: Perfect fit - Strong match across all areas
- **7-8**: Good fit - Strong academic/athletic match
- **5-6**: Moderate fit - Some areas align well
- **3-4**: Low fit - Few areas match
- **1-2**: Poor fit - Minimal alignment

Fit Score improves as you fill out your profile with academic and athletic info.

### Responsiveness Score

Shows how quickly a coach responds to you:
- **High**: Coach replies within 1-3 days
- **Medium**: Coach replies in 1-2 weeks
- **Low**: Coach takes 2+ weeks or doesn't respond

Use this to prioritize your recruiting efforts.

### Priority Tiers

Organize schools by likelihood and preference:

- **Reach Schools**: Dream schools (competitive, less likely)
- **Target Schools**: Realistic matches (good fit, competitive)
- **Safety Schools**: Likely admits (good fit, strong chance)

A balanced list typically has: 2-3 reach, 4-6 target, 2-3 safety schools.

## Troubleshooting

### Account & Login
- **Can't sign up?** Check your email for a confirmation link. Resend confirmation email from login page.
- **Forgot password?** Click "Forgot Password" on login page. Check your email for reset link.

### Schools & Coaches
- **Can't find my school?** Try variations of the name (e.g., "University of Florida" vs "Univ of Florida"). Email support if it's missing.
- **Coach info is wrong?** Edit the coach's cardâ€”click the pencil icon and update details.

### Interactions
- **Can't log an old interaction?** Click the date fieldâ€”you can set any past date.
- **Accidentally logged an interaction?** Click the three-dot menu on the interaction card and select "Delete."

### Profile & Data
- **My fit score seems low.** Complete your academic/athletic profileâ€”fit scores improve with more data.
- **Can't add parent?** Ensure you entered their correct email. They may need to check spam folder for invite.
